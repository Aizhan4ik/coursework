Dual Degree Programme

Earn two diplomas from Ala-Too International University and our partner institutions

Introduction

This document provides guidance for students of Ala-Too International University on how to apply for dual degree programmes. Students will learn about eligibility criteria and application procedures. The document also highlights the responsibilities of all stakeholders in the programme: students, home and host institutions.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Second year student
  • English language certificate: B2+ (room A-214) / IELTS 6+ / TOEFL PPT 520 / TOEFL IBT 72 / Cambridge Advanced Certificate grade A
  • No failed department core courses
  • GPA score: 89+

Selection Criteria

  • Candidates will be selected on the basis of submitted documents
  • Interview: February 28, 15:00

Required Documents

  • Online Application form
  • Official Transcript from Office of Student Affairs
  • Copy of International Passport
  • Certificate of English Language Skills
  • Motivation Letter
  • Recommendation letters (2)

Application Procedure

  1. Applicants can participate in a Briefing Seminar to get detailed information
  2. Submit online application with required documents by February 18
  3. Successful applicants will be invited to interview
  4. Applicants must be nominated to host University by March 10
  5. Submit additional documents required by host university
  6. Choose disciplines with assistance from Head of Departments

Upon Completion

  • Receive two diploma certificates
  • Receive two grade transcripts
  • Receive two diploma supplements

Financial Information

Students must pay individually for:

  • Visa (application, resubmission or reissue)
  • Flights
  • Insurance
  • Dormitory
  • Food
  • Living expenses
  • Learning materials

Note: Dual students must register and pay tuition and other required fees at their current institution.
