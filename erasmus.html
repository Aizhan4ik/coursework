Erasmus+ KA171 Program

International Credit Mobility sponsored by the European Union!

Program Overview

Erasmus+ KA171 is the International Credit Mobility Program sponsored by the European Union where participants get academic, personal and cultural development at one of the European universities with the benefit of 800/850 euros monthly and 820 euros for travel expenses.

AIU students who have excellent academic achievements and good level of English can participate in the competition and successful candidates will be awarded with Erasmus grants.

Program Benefits

  • Monthly grant of 800-850€
  • Travel allowance of 820€
  • Academic development at European universities
  • Cultural exchange opportunities

Selection Process

First Stage: Document Review

Conducted by AIU International Relations Office based on:

  • Eligibility criteria
  • Academic excellence
  • Motivation
  • Proficiency of English

Eligibility Criteria

  • GPA score — 87+
  • English Language level — C1+
  • Cannot be enrolled in first or last semester
  • Not a previous Erasmus+ KA171 grantee

Required Documents

  • Application Form
  • Official Transcript of records
  • English Language certificate (TOEFL IBT 80+, PBT 550, IELTS 6+)
  • Motivation Letter
  • Scan of passport

Second Stage: Interview

Conducted by Selection Committee (Rector, Vice Rectors, IRO Officers)

Selection Criteria:

  • Academic capacity (30%)
  • Motivation letter (30%)
  • Interview performance (40%)

Additional Requirements

For Nominated Students

  • Application form of receiving university
  • Learning Agreement (80% course match required)
  • Language Certificate
  • Proof of enrolment

Application Deadlines

  • Spring semester — November 30th
  • Fall semester – May 15th

Inclusive Education Initiative

Special Opportunities

AIU reserves 10% of Erasmus+ grants for students with fewer opportunities:

  • Students with disabilities
  • Students with economic disadvantages
  • Students from ethnic minority groups
  • Children of migrants/refugees
  • Students from remote/rural areas

Support Measures

  • Simplified application procedures
  • Additional preparation support
  • Mentorship programs
  • Assistance with visa and accommodation
  • Cultural orientation

Eligibility for Special Consideration

  • Belong to one of the above groups
  • English language level B2 minimum
  • Submit basic application documents
